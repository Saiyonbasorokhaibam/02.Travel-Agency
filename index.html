Manipur is a land of breathtaking beauty and vibrant culture. Planning the perfect vacation here means exploring lush green valleys, misty hills, serene lakes, and colorful traditions. From the floating islands of Loktak Lake to the rhythmic beats of Manipuri dance, every corner of the state offers something new to experience and cherish.

Top Attractions in Manipur

1. Loktak Lake, Manipur

Loktak Lake is the largest freshwater lake in Northeast India and one of the most enchanting natural wonders of Manipur. Its unique feature is the phumdis — floating islands made of vegetation and organic matter.

Top Attractions

  • Keibul Lamjao National Park
  • Sangai Deer
  • Phumdis (Floating Islands)

2. Kangla Fort

Located in the heart of Imphal, Kangla Fort stands as a proud symbol of Manipur’s glorious past. Once the ancient capital of the Meitei rulers, the fort served as the political and spiritual center of the Manipur kingdom for centuries.

Visit here for more

Top Attractions

  • Kangla Sha
  • Govindajee Temple
  • Kangla Museum
  • Photography and Sightseeing

3. Shirui Hills, Manipur

Perched high above the clouds in Ukhrul district, the Shirui Hills are home to the legendary Shirui Lily (Lilium mackliniae) — found nowhere else on Earth.

Top Attractions

  • Shirui Peak Trek

  • Shirui Lily Festival
